# Claim 0030: Item Anchor with Relational Ownership

## Statement

The stable node is `item`. Ownership, desire, custody, lending, and disposal
are modeled through relations and states around the item.

## Scope

- Collection naming must not bake ownership status into identity.
- Ownership and desire are represented as graph semantics, not collection names.

## Acceptance Checks

- Core schema uses `item` as the anchor.
- Ownership-like lifecycle concerns are modeled relationally.
