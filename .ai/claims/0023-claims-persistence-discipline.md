# Claim 0023: Claims Persistence Discipline

## Statement

When the user says to remember something, or when a definition is clearly
settled, the decision must be persisted into repo claims.

## Rules

- Explicit "remember" requests require claim updates.
- Settled definitions may be added to claims proactively.
- Claims should be updated in the relevant topic file as soon as the decision is
  stable enough to matter.

## Acceptance Checks

- Important decisions are reflected in topic claim indexes.
- Repo claim state stays aligned with settled architecture discussions.
