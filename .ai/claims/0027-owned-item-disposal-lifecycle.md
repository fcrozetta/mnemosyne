# Claim 0027: Item Disposition Lifecycle

## Statement

Items support disposition lifecycle without deletion, including practical
reasons such as sold, donated, lost, broken, too_small, and too_big.

## Scope

- Retain item history even after disposal.
- Record reason and timing for leaving active ownership.

## Acceptance Checks

- Items have non-destructive disposition state/history.
- Disposition reason remains queryable over time.
